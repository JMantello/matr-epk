About

We are a high-energy alt-rock band from Seattle, WA, bringing our Midwest emo roots to the moody PNW. We‘re currently looking to bring our energetic, dynamic sound to the stage and perform our hearts out in front of lively crowds. Our music draws on influences from bands like The Story So Far, The All-American Rejects, and Catfish and the Bottlemen.

Members:

  • Levi Malterud (Guitar, Vocals)
  • Austin Maier (Guitar)
  • Madison Albert (Bass, Vocals)
  • Jonathan Mantello (Drums)

We have released several singles and an EP titled Heartache - EP (2025), including tracks such as "Never Coming Back" (2024), "All You Talk About" (2025), and "Your Drive Home" (2025), and we’re working on completing our first album. We are active in the Seattle music scene and have performed at local venues like the Skylark Cafe and Tim’s Tavern.
